$1,000 Off Your First Month

When you contact West Linn Care directly — rather than through a placement agency — we pass the savings on to you. That means $1,000 off your first month of care, simply for reaching out to us yourself.

Why We Offer This Discount

Many families find adult foster homes through placement agencies. These agencies charge referral fees that increase the cost of care. When you come to us directly, we avoid those fees entirely — and we share that savings with you.

It's simple: no middleman means a better price for your family, with the exact same quality of care.

How It Works

1

Contact Us Directly

Call us at (503) 387-5317, email ken@westlinncare.com, or fill out our contact form.

2

Tour Our Home

Schedule a visit to see our home, meet our caregivers, and ask any questions you may have.

3

Save $1,000

When your loved one moves in, you'll receive $1,000 off the first month's rate. No codes, no catches.

Who Qualifies?

This discount is available to any new resident whose family contacts West Linn Care directly — without going through a third-party placement agency or referral service. That's the only requirement.

Whether you found us through a web search, a friend's recommendation, or by driving past our home, you qualify for this discount.

The Same Exceptional Care

This discount doesn't change a thing about the care your loved one receives. Every resident at West Linn Care enjoys:

  • 24-hour personalized care with a maximum of five residents
  • Private rooms with half bathrooms
  • Home-cooked meals tailored to individual preferences
  • Consistent, dedicated caregivers
  • A real home in a quiet West Linn neighborhood
  • Spacious outdoor areas and comfortable common spaces
