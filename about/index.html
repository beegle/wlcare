What is an Adult Foster Home?

An adult foster home is a residential care option that provides 24-hour care in a real home environment. Unlike large assisted living facilities, adult foster homes are limited to just five residents, ensuring personalized attention and a true sense of family.

Intimate Setting

With only five residents per home, each person receives individualized care and attention that simply isn't possible in larger facilities.

True Home Environment

Our home is a real residential house in a quiet neighborhood—not an institutional building. Residents enjoy home-cooked meals, comfortable furnishings, and a peaceful atmosphere.

Consistent Caregivers

Residents see the same familiar faces each day, building meaningful relationships with their caregivers rather than rotating staff.

Family Atmosphere

We create a family-like environment where residents feel valued, respected, and truly at home.

The Benefits of Adult Foster Care

  • Personalized, one-on-one attention
  • Home-cooked meals tailored to preferences
  • Quiet, peaceful residential neighborhood
  • Strong bonds with caregivers and fellow residents
  • Flexible routines that respect individual needs
  • More affordable than many larger facilities

Daily Life & Activities

Daily activities are essential to our residents' well-being—supporting physical health, mental sharpness, social connection, and emotional expression.

At West Linn Care, each day offers opportunities tailored to individual interests: puzzles and games to keep minds active, gentle stretching and walks for mobility, arts and crafts for creativity, gardening and outdoor time for fresh air, and shared meals and conversations for meaningful connection.

We follow each resident's lead. Some days are for activity, others for quiet rest. We always respect personal preferences while encouraging engagement that brings joy and purpose.
