Conveniently Located in West Linn, Oregon

West Linn Care is located at 1560 Rosemont Road in the heart of West Linn, Oregon. Our home sits in a quiet, tree-lined residential neighborhood that feels worlds away from the bustle of commercial districts—yet we're conveniently accessible from communities throughout Clackamas County and the southern Portland metro area.

We welcome residents and their families from the following communities:

West Linn

Our home community. West Linn Care is nestled in one of the city's peaceful residential neighborhoods, offering easy access for local families. Residents stay connected to their community while receiving personalized care.

About Our Neighborhood

Our Rosemont Road location offers the perfect balance of tranquility and accessibility. The neighborhood features mature trees, well-kept homes, and quiet streets—ideal for the peaceful environment our residents deserve. Nearby parks and green spaces provide beautiful scenery for drives and outings.
